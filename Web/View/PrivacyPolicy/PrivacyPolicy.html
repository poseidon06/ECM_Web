  • Welcome to BeautyZenith ("us," "we," or "our"). We value your trust and are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you visit and use our website to purchase and import cosmetic products from China.

  • Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: When you make a purchase on our website, we collect information such as your Name, Billing and Shipping Addresses, E-mail Address, Phone Number, and Payment Information.
